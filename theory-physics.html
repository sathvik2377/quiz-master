Physics Theory

Understand the fundamental laws of nature

Mechanics

Kinematics

  • Equations of Motion:
  • v = u + at
  • s = ut + ½at²
  • v² = u² + 2as
  • Projectile Motion:
  • Range: R = u²sin2θ/g
  • Maximum height: H = u²sin²θ/2g
  • Time of flight: T = 2usinθ/g

Laws of Motion

  • Newton's First Law: Law of Inertia
  • Newton's Second Law: F = ma
  • Newton's Third Law: Action-Reaction pairs
  • Friction: f = μN

Work, Energy and Power

  • Work: W = F·s = Fs cosθ
  • Kinetic Energy: KE = ½mv²
  • Potential Energy: PE = mgh
  • Power: P = W/t = F·v
  • Work-Energy Theorem: W = ΔKE

Thermodynamics

Laws of Thermodynamics

  • Zeroth Law: Thermal equilibrium
  • First Law: ΔU = Q - W
  • Second Law: Entropy always increases
  • Third Law: Entropy approaches zero at absolute zero

Heat Transfer

  • Conduction: Q = kA(ΔT/Δx)t
  • Convection: Natural and forced
  • Radiation: Stefan-Boltzmann law

Kinetic Theory of Gases

  • Ideal Gas Law: PV = nRT
  • Average KE: ½mv² = (3/2)kT
  • RMS Speed: v_rms = √(3RT/M)

Waves and Oscillations

Simple Harmonic Motion

  • Displacement: x = A sin(ωt + φ)
  • Velocity: v = Aω cos(ωt + φ)
  • Acceleration: a = -Aω² sin(ωt + φ)
  • Time Period: T = 2π/ω
  • Frequency: f = 1/T = ω/2π

Wave Motion

  • Wave Equation: y = A sin(kx - ωt)
  • Wave Speed: v = fλ = ω/k
  • Doppler Effect: f' = f(v ± v_o)/(v ± v_s)

Sound Waves

  • Speed in air: v = 331 + 0.6T (m/s)
  • Intensity: I = P/A
  • Decibel scale: β = 10 log(I/I₀)

Electricity and Magnetism

Electrostatics

  • Coulomb's Law: F = kq₁q₂/r²
  • Electric Field: E = F/q = kQ/r²
  • Electric Potential: V = kQ/r
  • Capacitance: C = Q/V
  • Energy: U = ½CV² = ½QV

Current Electricity

  • Ohm's Law: V = IR
  • Power: P = VI = I²R = V²/R
  • Resistors in Series: R_total = R₁ + R₂ + ...
  • Resistors in Parallel: 1/R_total = 1/R₁ + 1/R₂ + ...

Magnetism

  • Magnetic Force: F = qvB sinθ
  • Biot-Savart Law: dB = (μ₀/4π)(I dl × r)/r³
  • Ampere's Law: ∮B·dl = μ₀I
  • Faraday's Law: ε = -dΦ/dt

Optics

Geometric Optics

  • Snell's Law: n₁sinθ₁ = n₂sinθ₂
  • Mirror Formula: 1/f = 1/u + 1/v
  • Lens Formula: 1/f = 1/v - 1/u
  • Magnification: m = v/u = h'/h

Wave Optics

  • Young's Double Slit: β = λD/d
  • Single Slit Diffraction: a sinθ = nλ
  • Grating Equation: d sinθ = nλ
  • Polarization: Malus' Law: I = I₀cos²θ

Modern Physics

Quantum Physics

  • Planck's Equation: E = hf
  • Photoelectric Effect: hf = φ + KE_max
  • de Broglie Wavelength: λ = h/p
  • Uncertainty Principle: Δx·Δp ≥ h/4π

Atomic Physics

  • Bohr's Model: E_n = -13.6/n² eV
  • Rydberg Formula: 1/λ = R(1/n₁² - 1/n₂²)
  • X-ray Production: λ_min = hc/eV

Nuclear Physics

  • Mass-Energy: E = mc²
  • Radioactive Decay: N = N₀e^(-λt)
  • Half-life: t₁/₂ = ln2/λ
  • Binding Energy: BE = (Δm)c²
